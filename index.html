About GTRC

Some of us came from NYRR group training, others didn't. We're a community of people who love to run even if running doesn’t always love us back. We run and play together. We cheer for each other, support each other's goals and are always pushing towards our best selves. We’re committed to fun and we drink coffee, like a lot of coffee.
